Prairie Oyster

Description

Get ready for jazzy space adventure!

Ingredients

  • one egg yolk
  • a pinch of salt and pepper
  • two servings of Worcestershire sauce
  • one portion of hot sauce (Tabasco or Cholula)
  • 30 ml of gin

Steps

  1. Add an egg yolk to a small glass without breaking it
  2. Put in a pinch of salt and a little black pepper
  3. Combine with a couple of dashes of Worcestershire sauce
  4. Add one dash of hot sauce
  5. Pour gin

Cowboy

Description

*aims gun* — *fires twice* — *blows smoke off the barrel*

Ingredients

  • 30 ml of Half-and-Half mixture (cream and milk)
  • 60 ml of bourbon

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients
  2. Shake the mixture over ice
  3. Strain into a cocktail glass ('cause you're always lone)

Vodka Toddy

Description

Drown your sorrow

Ingredients

  • 60 ml of vodka
  • 120 ml of hot water
  • 15 ml of rosemary simple syrup
  • thin slices of lemon
  • sprig of fresh rosemary (for decoration)

Steps

  1. Add vodka to a glass
  2. Pour in hot water
  3. Add rosemary simple syrup and thin slices of lemon
  4. Garnish with a sprig of rosemary
