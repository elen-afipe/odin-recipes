Spirit Bomb

Description

You're a master of revolution, baby

Ingredients

  • 30 ml. lime juice
  • 15 ml. blue Curaçao
  • 15 ml. simple syrup
  • 15 ml. italicus liqueur

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to the cocktail shaker
  2. Fill it with ice, seal than shake vigorously
  3. Carbonate using a siphon (if possible)

Tequila Sunset

Description

Disco inferno!

Ingredients

  • 60 ml. tequila
  • 120 ml. orange juice
  • a drop of blackberry brandy liqueur
  • orange and mint (for decoration)

Steps

  1. Add first two ingredients to the cocktail shaker
  2. Fill it with ice, seal than shake vigorously
  3. Pour into a glass and add a bit of liqueur
  4. Garnish with orange and mint

Sunrise Parabellum

Description

Revolutionary speaking

Ingredients

  • 15 ml. simple syrup
  • 22 ml. Dry Curaçao
  • 15 ml. Chartreuse liqueur
  • 45 ml. coffee liqueur
  • 15 ml. scotch
  • orange peel (for decoration)

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to the glass
  2. Fill it with ice, than mix
  3. Pour into a glass and add a bit of liqueur
  4. Garnish with burnt orange peel
